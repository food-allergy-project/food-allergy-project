Casual User Persona

Name: Sarah C. Smith
Age:25

Profession: Sarah works as a full time nurse at a local hospital down the road from where she lives. She recently came across a middle age patient at work who just found out she was allergic to dairy and soy. Sarah thought she might want to get a check on herself, as she didn't know you can get allergies in the middle of your life. She ended up finding out she is too allergic to soy.

Technology: Sarah is windows casual user, she uses a Windows desktop at home running the new Windows 11 update, she prefers to use firefox as her default web browser. She also uses an ipad for when she's out and about, or while she's just on the couch, she uses the default browser for the ipad (safari). Sarah's phone is a samsung galaxy 12 running the latest update, and using chrome as her phones browser.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Sarah doesn't have too much free time, she works a ton and is usually exhausted after work and will just eat and get some sleep after a long day at work. Sarah's tried of looking at different recipe websites that have a huge list of recipes that's overwhelming and confusing to read. she's tried of clicking on one recipe she might like only to be redirected to some new website that has nothing to do with what she is looking for.

Frustrations and Needs: Sarah needs to find a website/app that she can use as to find recipes that cater to her allergy. Sarah frustrated from clicking on one recipe she might like only to be redirected to some new website that has nothing to do with what she is looking for. She needs an easy-to-use website that won't take her all over the internet. Sarah also wants to be able to pick if It's something for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or just a snack she can quickly make. Sarah wants to be able to save recipes that she's liked in the past and be able to go back to them for later.

Goals: Sarah is starting to fixate on finding this type of site for her, and wants to be able to use this site every day. Sarah also want's to be able to just find a recipe fast and easy, be able to save it for next time, and see other top voted recipes.

User Story: As a casual user, I need to be able to determine quickly what recipe I can cook for breakfast, lunch, or dinner that fits my allergy needs.
