Capstone Project: Allergic Foodies


Executive Summary

The Problem

According to www.foodallergy.org, researchers estimated that 32 million Americans are living with potentially life-threatening food allergies. This includes 5.6 million children under 18 years of age, which is one in every 13 children. In addition, about 40 percent of people with food allergies are allergic to more than one food. Living with food allergies is not easy, and it can be a hassle and time-consuming trying to find recipes that are free of your specific allergens.

How We Solve It

Our website will offer a quick and easy way for people with food allergies to find recipes that are free from their allergens. We will do the following:

  • Quickly generate a personalized homepage with recipe recommendations for people that are suffering from specific food allergies
  • Present menu ideas for breakfast, lunch, dinner, dessert, and snack that are free of users' allergens
  • Present users with food substitution ideas
  • Allow users to favorite/save/sort recipes
  • Allow comments on recipes from users, so users can exchange ideas

Key Next Steps & Final Thoughts

Our key next steps:

  • Gathering information: target audience, main goals, etc.
  • Planning & Designing: wireframe creation and color scheme selection
  • Content Assembly
  • Coding
  • Test & Review, and Launch

Our website will not only allow users to save their time searching for recipes that they can eat - it will also let users comment on recipes to exchange ideas and opinions. Being able to easily find recipes that are free from your allergens is crucial when living with food allergies! Whether you're allergic to one food or several, our website can quickly generate recipes that you and your whole family can enjoy!
