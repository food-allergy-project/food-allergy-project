Usecase and Interaction Flow:

Usecase

Megan's made a gluten-free recipe for orange chicken on her feed. She wants to comment on it and share with users how it turned out.

pre-condition:Megan is on home page of Allergicfoodie and logged in

post-condition:Megan commented on the recipe she enjoyed

Interaction flow:

Megan is on the home page of Allergicfoodie and clicks on her profile

Allergicfoodie takes her to her profile

Megan clicks on her favorited recipes

Allergic foodie takes her to all of her favorited gluten-free recipes

Megan scrolls down and clicks on the gluten-free Orange chicken recipe

Allergicfoodie takes her to a page that displays the recipes as well as comments below

Megan scrolls down and clicks on comment

Allergicfoodie brings up a window that says "What would you like to say about this recipe?"

Megan comments "this recipe was one of my favorites on Allergicfoodie. It tasted like it was from a restaurant and I could not even be able to tell it was gluten-free if I didn't know!" she then clicks on the submit comment button

Allergicfoodie posts her comment at the bottom for recent comments for all users to see
