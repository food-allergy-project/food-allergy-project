Wireframe: Personalized Homepage

Content Strategy

This page will be a personalized homepage where the user can see suggested recipes that are free from their selected allergens.

Header: The header will include a logo of Allergic Foodies.

Nav Bar & Post A Recipe Feature: This section will include links to “My Homepage”, “My Account”, and “My Favorites” as well as a button for users to be able to post their recipe on the website.

Welcome/Profile Overview: This section will have a short welcome message as well as the user’s profile picture and summary of what they’re allergic to.

Recipe Categories: his section will display five different categories: breakfast, lunch, dinner, desserts, and snacks. This will allow the user to be able to quickly pick meal categories of their choice. When selecting one or more of these categories, the “Suggested Recipes” section will change accordingly.

Suggested Recipes: This section will display ten suggested recipes that the user can make according to their allergy or allergies. Users can click the “Refresh” button in the top right corner of this section to get new ten suggested recipes.

Footer: The footer will contain credits (created by (names)) or other additional information we may add.
