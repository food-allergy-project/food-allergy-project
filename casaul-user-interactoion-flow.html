Casual User's Interaction Flow

Role: Casual User

As a causal user of this site(website name) I want to be able to find new recipes that cater to my allergies.

Precondition

User has an account already made, and is ready to take Food Quiz.

Post condition

User see a list of recipes that she's able to make, and safely eat.

Usecase

Sarah's just recently found out she can no longer eat any type of soy, she's been constantly thinking of what she will be able to cook, and what recipe she will be able to use. She's hoping she will be able to find something on the internet that suits her needs fast.

Interaction Flow

  • Sarah is logged in the website and ready to take test
  • AllergicFoodie home page shows a "take quiz" button in the middle of the screen
  • Sarah clicks on "take quiz"
  • AllergicFoodie responds by opening the quiz page
  • Sarah clicks on one of the type allergy's she has
  • AllergicFoodie responds by highlighting that selected allergy
  • Sarah now clicks on the other allergy she has
  • AllergicFoodie responds by again highlighting the newly selected allergy
  • Sarah clicks on submit quiz
  • AllergicFoodie responds by opening a list of recipes that is taking in account for the selected allergy's
  • Sarah clicks on a recipe she likes and opens it.
